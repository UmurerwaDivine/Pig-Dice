Pig Dice Game

Rules

The rules of the game are:

  • when we press roll dice your score will be added in total score
  • when a player rolls 1 will be alerted and reverts to the next player
  • when a player press hold your score is added to total and reverts to the next player
  • when a player scores 100 points wil be the winner of the game

Good luck Both
